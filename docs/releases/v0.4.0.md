# Lantern Tide v0.4.0 — The doors open sooner

The island has learned to expect you. Step onto the street and, quietly in the background, the shops ready their rooms — so when you choose one, the door is already open. And on the days the tide runs slow, the light no longer waits frozen: it keeps breathing softly until you're through.

This is a Vibe Code project — made to let a passing idea keep breathing, not to be a flawless codebase. Treat its words as a small light drifting in on the tide, not a source to rely on. The Stillroom is a place to ease the mind, not medical care.

Also available in: [Traditional Chinese](./v0.4.0.zh-Hant.md)

## The doors open sooner

Before, the first time you stepped into a shop on a cold visit, you might wait a few breaths at the threshold while the room gathered itself. Now the street quietly warms the rooms the moment you arrive, so stepping in is all but instant. Touch a storefront and it lights at once — a small amber glow, so you know the door is opening even before the room appears. And in the tunnel, if the way is slow to clear, the light keeps its gentle breath instead of holding still; you're never left staring at a frozen frame.

Nothing about the ritual changed — the walk in from the tide is as unhurried as ever. It's only the waiting that has gone soft.

## A note under the lamp

This is still an early light, lit on a passing thought and kept burning only while it glows. Nothing here is a promise — rooms, words, and small details may still shift from one visit to the next. Take what helps you, and let the rest drift on with the tide.
